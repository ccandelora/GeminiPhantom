Frequently Asked Questions

How does Mystic Insights work?

Our readings are generated by AI for entertainment purposes only. They should not be considered as actual psychic or spiritual guidance.

Yes, registered users can view their past sessions in their account dashboard.

Currently, Mystic Insights is free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but we'll always offer a free tier.
